About Me





I’m Gavin Garcia, a multi-disciplinary designer living in San Antonio, TX. I’ve been designing since before I knew what to even call it, from designing Myspace graphics to creating Tumblr layouts for my blog. While in the process of researching my passion for design and coding I stumbled upon UX/UI Design. My passion personified into a career that I’m now pursuing for a living. While I’ve dabbled in both these subjects for some time, I’m happy learning almost anything as long as I’m helping to solve a viable problem.
